Protect, Report, Connect!

Eco Guardian enables Ugandan community members to report environmental issues such as deforestation, pollution, and illegal dumping directly to local authorities. Users can take pictures, provide descriptions, and share the location of incidents. This information is sent to local LC5 authorities, who then communicate with LC1 and other relevant bodies to address the issues.

Eco Guardian crowdsources environmental reports to help track and mitigate ecological problems in Uganda. The app facilitates direct communication between the community and local authorities, making it easier to report and resolve environmental issues promptly.

Eco Guardian is developed by the Eco Guardian Initiative with support from various Ugandan environmental organizations and community groups.
