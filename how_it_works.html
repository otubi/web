Submit Environmental Reports

Install the Eco Guardian app on your mobile devices. At the first launch, the app will guide you through the settings to customize notifications and permissions.

When you notice an environmental issue like deforestation, pollution, or illegal dumping, open the app to report it. Take a picture, provide a description, and share the location of the incident. This information will be sent to the local LC5 authority, who can then communicate with LC1 and other relevant bodies to address the issue.

Once submitted, your report will include the location, date, time, and description of the incident. This data is compiled and sent to authorities to facilitate prompt action.

Visualize Reports

Your report will be added to the public Eco Guardian interactive map. Icons on the map represent different types of reports, and clicking on them will show the details of each report, including the date, time, description, and location.

You can explore the map by panning and zooming to see various reports across different regions. This helps in understanding the spread and impact of environmental issues in your community.

The timeline feature at the bottom of the map allows you to view reports from different dates and compare data over time. Darker colors indicate a higher number of reports for that day.
